The website you saw earlier expresses the process of falling into narrow-minded thinking because of algorithms.
The world seen through algorithms is convenient, but it can prevent the user from seeing various aspects of the world.
There is a proverb called 'The frog in the Well' in Korea. It is a sarcastic remark about a person who thinks himself/herself superior even though he/she knows a tiny part of the world.

One day I realized algorithms were producing a lot of frogs in this world. And I thought I was also one of those frogs.
I anticipated it would be nice to communicate this situation to others and talk about it with each other, so I started this project. I would be happy if you were interested in the previous website and started to think about this problem. Feel free to send e-mail if you have any new ideas or supplements for this website.
